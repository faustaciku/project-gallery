Dictionary App

I have created this dictionary app with React. I used Pexels and Dictionary API's. You can look up any word and phonetics, meaning, synonyms and even photos regarding that word will show up. I have also included a react-audio-player to listen to word's pronunciation.

Personal Website

This is my first personal website. I have added a working navbar by using Bootstrap, my logo and a total of 4 pages: homepage, about me, my projects and contact page. I have added images and links of my first projects, a short description about me and my contact information.

Tribute Page

My tribute page is about one of the most inspiring people - Jacque Fresco. I have included his biography, images, most fascinating Venus project, qoutes and a list of books written by him. It is fully responsive and has a current date displayed in a top left corner (using Javascript).

Weather App

This weather app was created using React. I used Open Weather Map API for this project. You can search for any city in the world and it will display an icon, temperature, humidity and wind speen for the current day and forecast for 7 days. Current day and time is also displayed using Javascript.

This project was designed and coded by Fausta and it is open-sourced on Github.
